Correlation between air pollution and bus traffic

This map shows that sensors located very close to high bus traffic streets (50m radius) had a bigger gap between the lowest and the highest air pollution values.

The circle radius shows how big was the daily gap between lowest and highest air pollution value.

Without bus traffic near the sensor, the circle shows a solid green colour.

More the traffic, reddish the circle become. The bus traffic value is normalized with it's week peak.

Data collected between 25/01/2015 and 31/01/2015, an extremely hot week in Rio, with heavy rains at 18:00. It helps the air pollutants natural transportation to high layers of the atmosphere, decreasing city pollution.
Clicking on the marks gives some info about the sensor and the collected data.
